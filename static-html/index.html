Ask me about COVID-19

Hi, I’m your HealthBuddy. Just contact me via the chat window and I will give you information about the novel coronavirus disease (COVID-19). Ask anything about the virus, the disease and the pandemic and I will find the information for you, from the trusted sources of WHO and UNICEF. Before you start, here are the different options you have for talking to me:


1. Select your language: I’ll detect the language setting of your browser and, if available, will automatically speak in that language. You can also change the chat language from the language dropdown menu at the top of this web page.

1. Selecione seu idioma: Irei detectar a configuração de idioma do seu navegador e, se disponível, falarei automaticamente nesse idioma. Você também pode alterar o idioma do bate-papo no menu suspenso de idiomas, na parte superior desta página da web.


2. Select from the list of common questions: To get a quick answer, you can select from the list of common questions that I display in the chat window.

2. Selecione na lista de perguntas comuns: Para obter uma resposta rápida, você pode selecionar na lista de perguntas comuns que eu mostro na janela de bate-papo.


3. Send me your questions in natural language: Type in your question(s) in the chat window. Remember to write as if you are talking to your friend so I can understand and answer better.

3. Envie-me suas perguntas em idioma natural: Digite suas perguntas na janela de bate-papo. Lembre-se de escrever como se estivesse conversando com seu amigo para que eu possa entender e responder melhor.

What is HealthBuddy

HealthBuddy is a joint initiative of the WHO Regional Office for Europe and UNICEF’s Europe and Central Asia Regional Office. It is available on multiple social media and instant messaging platforms for users around the world to provide them with scientific and evidence-based advice. There is a lot of misinformation and myths about the new coronavirus (COVID-19 virus) circulating on the Internet and in social media. Misinformation is one of the biggest challenges in fighting COVID-19. This is why it’s important to be careful where you look for information and advice.
