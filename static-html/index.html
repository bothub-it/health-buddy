About HealthBuddy

There is a lot of misinformation and myths about the new coronavirus (COVID-19 virus) circulating on the Internet and in social media. Misinformation is one of the biggest challenges in fighting COVID-19. This is why it’s important to be careful where you look for information and advice.

Multi-channel support

HealthBuddy is available on multiple social media and instant messaging platforms in Europe to provide users with scientific and evidence-based advice.

Multi-language support

HealthBuddy detects the language of your browser and, if supported, will automatically speak in that language. You can also change the language from the languages menu.

Accurate COVID-19 information

HealthBuddy is supported by experts from WHO and UNICEF and will provide you with accurate information on COVID-19.

Fighting rumours and fake news

If you hear any rumours or fake statements in your community or region, you can report it to us here. HealthBuddy will compile them and counter them here with scientific information.
