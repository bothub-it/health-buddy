What is Health Buddy

HealthBuddy is a powerful artificial intelligence tool that can help you to know about Covid-19 and learn how to protect yourself and others from it as per the evidence based guidelines from WHO and UNICEF. There are a lot of myths and misinformation about coronavirus which is being shared on the net and social media. So, it’s important to be careful where you look for information and advice. The virtual health advisor “Joy” will give you useful information on how to protect yourself and others and how reduce the risk of infection.
